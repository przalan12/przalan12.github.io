Challenges

One of the challenges I sometimes face when consuming media is the little control I have to influence the story. Whether that be in a TV show or video game, there are times when the content leaves me dissatisfied - wishing I could do something about it. As a content generator, I like to take a lot of photos and videos. However, a challenge I sometimes face is having to find the right photo to upload to social media. The abundance of captured moments often leads to a dilemma of selecting the most meaningful and visually appealing content, creating a balance between personal significance and audience engagement.
